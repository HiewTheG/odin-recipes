Pandan Layered Cake

Description

This is a traditional Malaysian cake made with pandan leaves, which gives it a unique green color and fragrant aroma. The cake is typically layered with coconut cream and has a soft, spongy texture.

Ingredients

  • 200g pandan leaves
  • 200g sugar
  • 200g flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 200ml water
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Blend pandan leaves with water to extract juice, strain and set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add flour, baking powder, and salt; mix well.
  5. Stir in coconut milk, pandan juice, and vanilla extract.
  6. Pour batter into a greased cake pan.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Let it cool before serving.
